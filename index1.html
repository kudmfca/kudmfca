Mission

Our mission at the Kingdom United Destiny Ministerial Fellowship of Churches Association (KUDMFCA) is to unite pastors and churches under a common purpose of advancing the Kingdom of God through collaboration, support, and spiritual growth. We are dedicated to empowering clergy, fostering unity within the body of Christ, and providing resources and guidance to ensure that our members are equipped to lead their congregations effectively. By promoting mutual respect, shared vision, and collective action, we aim to build a strong and vibrant network of Christian leaders who are committed to transforming lives and communities for the glory of God.

Vision

Our vision is to see a unified and empowered community of Christian leaders who, through collaboration and shared purpose, bring about spiritual renewal and positive change in their congregations and communities. We envision a fellowship where pastors and churches work together in harmony, reflecting the love and unity of Christ, and where each member is fully equipped to fulfill their God-given calling. Through our efforts, we strive to create a lasting impact on the world, advancing the Kingdom of God and glorifying His name in all that we do.
